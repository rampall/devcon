We at Status have been working to enable an interface to Web3 since our inception. We bought into the principles of this ecosystem, and have spent extraordinary effort to not compromise on those ethics while we continue to create applications that are easily accessible while also maintaining our user's rights. 

This talk is an overview of this journey, lessons learned, the fruits of this labor, why we're doubling down on this process, and why you should to.
