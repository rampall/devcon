As more ZK-enabled applications come online, we'll need to build a robust security ecosystem and new techniques to ensure that our ZK apps aren't vulnerable to attackers. In this session hosted by 0xPARC and Privacy and Scaling Explorations, specialists in ZK, formal verification, and assurance discuss new approaches to securing these next-generation decentralized apps.
