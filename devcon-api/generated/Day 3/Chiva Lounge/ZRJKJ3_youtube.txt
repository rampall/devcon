A friendly Jazz & Blues jam where we can play (easy) jazz standards & blues classics and Bossa Nova. We invite all jazz singers, pianists, guitarists, ukulelists, percussionists or any other instrument to join us  on stage. So don’t be shy, always wanted to sing your favourite Sinatra song or play any instrument and know some basic jazz songs like that just hop on. We will support you as much as we can. We will use some sheet music and chord apps to make it happen.
